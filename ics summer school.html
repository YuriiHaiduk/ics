Program

Introduction

In the last years a lot of successful applications of machine learning and deep learning have appeared. It will be expected that data driven approaches will revolutionize medicine, e.g. for diagnosis. Autonomous driving will change mobility dramatically. During the summer school the students deepen their knowledge in Machine Learning and they will work on practical (research) problems/applications.

Academic and didactic concept

1. In the morning there are lectures (including exercises) on fundamentals and advanced topics of machine and deep learning.

2. In the afternoon the students work on a special topics and applications depending on their interest in mixed international groups. They will work on projects to deepen their theoretical and/or practical knowledge.
